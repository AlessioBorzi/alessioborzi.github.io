I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in the Nonlinear Algebra group led by Bernd Sturmfels.

Before moving to the MPI, I did my PhD studies at the University of Warwick under the supervision of Diane Maclagan.

Previously, I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at the University of Catania, my advisor there was Marco D'Anna. I was also a student of the Scuola Superiore di Catania.
